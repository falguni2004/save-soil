Oberoi Mall partners with ‘Save Soil’ movement

On the occasion of World Environment Day, Oberoi Mall collaborated with the ‘Save Soil’ movement which addresses the global soil crisis and raises awareness about different ways to protect soil. ‘Save Soil’ is a global movement to address land degradation and advocate for healthy soil. As part of the initiative, Oberoi Mall conducted a campaign to educate patrons about the agricultural soil degradation issue and the need for measures to increase green cover across the globe through afforestation.
